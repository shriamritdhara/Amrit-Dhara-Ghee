Our Story

From the Heart of
Indian Villages

Crafted with tradition, delivered with purity. Our ghee follows the same process grandmothers passed down for generations — from A2 cow milk, to curd, to golden bilona ghee.

01

Ethical Milking

Milk collected only after the calves are fed — respecting the natural bond.

02

Bilona Churning

Curd slow-churned with a wooden bilona to extract pure golden butter.

03

Slow Simmering

Butter simmered on low flame in clay pots until liquid gold remains.
